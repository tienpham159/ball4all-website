Vision

In a world where one’s passions are being overshadowed by mundanity, we believe that you deserve to find the balance between them, only then you can endure the benefits without commitment.

Brand Values

  • Flexibility: Between responsibilities and free time. In addition, we have chosen to follow a more laid back approach through our social media posts since we’d like to shed some light on the fun side of football.
  • Belonging: We would like to ensure that there is a safe environment for everyone who want to play the beautiful game regardless of skill.
  • Passion: We want to ensure that the passion for the beautiful sport is prioritized over the mundanity of day to day life.

    Design Elements

    • Color palette  
      • #FF8B00 According to color psychology, orange is often associated with feelings of enthusiasm and excitement. Because orange is a high-energy color, many sports teams use orange in their uniforms, mascots, and branding. We use orange to appeal to the primarily young demographic of our customer segment and express the playful, energetic side of our brand personality.
      • #889600 The color green relates to balance, growth, and a sense of community. Through this color, we want to convey the team spirit often seen in football games and the flexibility that our brand offers. It also represents the color of the football grassfield which aligns with the sport segment that we’re focusing on.
      • #323C13 #EDEAE4 Since our final products include a website and a mobile app, we chose dark green and beige instead of black and white to maintain the necessary contrast in colors but also heighten the website/app’s UX/UI, since black and white is reported to cause exhaustion if the user looks at the screen for an extend period of time.
    • Font choices
      The typeface If Kica in uppercase is utilized for our logo as well as headings for our campaign. The typeface design is horizontally wide with equal weight for each letter, which expresses energy, masculinity and modernity. As a unique display typeface, If Kica reflects and differentiates the brand’s personality from other brands. However, paying attention to the UX/UI aspect, we chose Poppins as the primary typeface for content-heavy designs. Poppins’ letters are designed with clean lines/circles within a square-shaped container, which is youthful but also maintains readability when displayed on website/mobile app/social platforms. This typeface also has a variety of weights and styles that allow versatility in design.

      • Marketing

      Context of campaign and promotional activities

      Our Brand

      At Ball4all, we are on a mission to help users create an easily accessible community amongst amateur football players. In doing research, we discovered that football is an extremely common and popular team sport in The Netherlands, therefore we decided to focus on football to obtain maximum engagement and strengthen a community that is already visible in The Netherlands. In a world where people often have incredibly busy schedules, we aim to provide an accommodating and flexible platform that allows users to still participate in their passion. Our campaign is not only about promoting a product, it is about allowing people to celebrate the joy of football.

      What is the Campaign About?

      The Ball4All campaign revolves around distributing content that builds excitement while introducing our brand features. Through our posts we aim to build a community in the football world, demonstrating how we plan to make their life easier while gaining their trust by incorporating real people’ opinions through street interviews. Through our campaign we aim to spark meaningful conversations and represent the individuals who make up the community of The Netherlands football scene.

      The Message

      The tagline for our campaign is “FWB. Football with Benefits. All the Benefits without Commitment”. Through the tagline our brand aims to demonstrate a company that provides features to help make a footballers experience and life easier, allowing them to access benefits of the sport without having to commit to a club. Our message is consistently represented throughout our content and social media platforms, including the tagline in videos and our bio, as well as having posts that further explain how we plan to execute our vision.

      Promotional Activities Executed
      To bring our message to life and connect with our audience, we've executed a variety of promotional activities:
    • Instagram and TikTok Content: We've launched engaging campaigns on Instagram and TikTok to showcase our brand and its features. Through street interviews, brand feature descriptions, brand collaborations and company introduction posts, we have worked towards inviting our viewers to become part of the growing community.
    • Brand Collaborations: We have reached out to other brands to collaborate and share each other's content. Through this process we attempted to gain brand connections while continuously promoting and growing our brand.
    • Interactive Components and Trends: We've launched interactive components on our stories in an attempt to once again make viewers feel they are connected with the brand and have a voice. In addition, we have posted videos following trends such as street interviews as well as using trending audios in our video to gain traction.
    • Hashtags: In each of our posts we ensured to include at least 3-4 hashtags in the caption. These often consisted of tags that correlated with our post, as well as tags that are popular in the football community.

      • The Objective - SMART

      (Specific) We want to grow brand awareness by creating different types of content on both Instagram and Tik Tok. Our content will feature street interviews where we go around the hotspots of Breda, interacting directly with our target audience. Moreover, in these interviews, we ask questions about their difficulties as an amateur football player, whilst also asking about the usability of our features. Therefore, by posting these kinds of videos, we predict that most of our target audience have faced the same problems, creating a sense of belonging. In addressing the difficulties of amateur football players and seeking feedback on our brand features, it will allow our team to create a relatable brand, while genuinely addressing our target audience's needs. Creating a foundation of trust when building a long-lasting community.

      (Measurable) Our goal would be to reach 3000 impressions on both platforms. Population of Breda (since we’re going to interact with it the most) 180.000. 6% of the Netherland’s population play football, extrapolating and applying it to the population of Breda, we have 10.800. 50% are males and given the fact our target audience is based on male amateur football players between the ages of 18-24, 3000 impressions is predicted to be an achievable goal.

      (Achievable) By reaching our goal, we will attract more customers towards the website/app, building a long-lasting community in time.

      (Timely) We’d like to achieve this goal within a month.

      The Selected Channels/Platforms
      We chose Instagram and TikTok as our channels due to the fact that they are the most relevant social media platforms for our target audience, male amateur football players aged 18-23. We got to this conclusion based on interviews with our target audience, all of them mentioning both. However, most of them use TikTok for entertainment and Instagram for both news and entertainment. Therefore, we chose to use Instagram for more “serious” posts such as our feature showcase, and TikTok for more funny, light-hearted content such as “types of footballers”. In addition, Instagram and TikTok are both known for their engaging formats, providing an ideal platform for sharing content and gaining viewers. The platforms are powerful tools in building brand visibility and reaching a large audience, providing our brand the opportunity to introduce our company to a variety of potential customers. Through the various features that platforms provide, such as stories, reels, reposts and short TikTok our team is provided flexibility to share our content in various forms. Including call-to-action in our posts and utilizing engagement features on our stories, it encourages a relationship with our audience. The platforms allow our team to experiment and discover which format captures our audience's attention most. Therefore, we utilized the platform's key benefits by incorporating trending audios, hashtags and many additional engagement tactics, in an attempt to reach our goal of 3000 impressions. The platforms provide a sense of community as it often caters to viewers interests, allowing football players in the Netherlands to discover our curated content for the sport.

    Future Planning

    In reflecting on our marketing journey, it is evident that our campaign has undergone significant growth as overtime we began to utilize more features on the platform to promote our content. However, it is evident that we did not reach our goal of 3000 impressions. If the project were to continue, we would be able to use the experience we have gained in the month of content creation, to implement a strategy aimed at further enhancing engagement and solidifying our brand identity. The following recommendation represents a detailed outline of how we would strive for future success.

    1. Transition to a Business Account: The first recommendation and change our team would implement is making our social media accounts a business profile from the beginning. Transforming our platforms into business accounts would allow us to have access to in-depth analytics, representing the growth our company has shown overtime and from the beginning. In having this tool we would be able to review engagement insights and reflect on what we can do each post to improve, such as posting at a different time/day, using different hashtags and discovering the type of content that our audience enjoys most.

    2. Strategic Hashtags: In using hashtags, we believe it has helped our brand to get engagement, therefore in the future of our campaign we would continue to utilize relevant and trending hashtags across both social media platforms. However, a change we would implement is doing further research on what hashtags are trending and incorporating a lot more hashtags than 4-5, in order to enhance the chance of viewers to discover our company.

Initially our team wanted to represent a more serious and cinematic approach to our content. In addition, we realized the importance of our tone which was incorporating slang versus professional text, which resonates more with our audience. Therefore, in creating a distinct brand voice, it allows us to connect more with our audience and establish a stronger brand identity.

On platforms such as TikTok, posting everyday allows a greater chance to reach viewers and appear on users' pages. In addition, posting more frequently allows viewers to engage with content and develop a stronger understanding and relationship of the brand.

    Overall, we believe in future planning, by following these future recommendations, discovered through research and content analytics, we believe we can redirect our campaign to increase visibility and growth.

    Justification 

      Customer segments
    •  Getting “team sports players” as our assigned audience group, we decided to narrow down the customer segment to male amateur football players age 18-30 living in the Netherlands, as we assumed having a unique target audience would benefit our brand in looking for specific problems as well as developing features that centralizes the target segment. Due to its widespread popularity in the Netherlands and community values, we decided to include football into our audience’s characteristics, and our brand targeted male amateur players since football is primarily male-dominated, and it’s been shown that the professional segment of football is already prioritized, which drove us to put emphasis on the amateur segments for unique problems and solutions.
    •  Problems From the customer segment, we determined three main problems that customers may face while playing amateur football, which were schedule flexibility, pitch booking, and finding teammates. Based on which, our initial solution was to develop a platform for male amateur football players that provide the services according to our assumed problems. Channels Our main channels would be our website and social media platforms including Tiktok and Instagram, since the chosen customer segment is primarily Gen Zs and Millenials whose screen time is spent mostly on Instagram and Tiktok. The social media platforms would be utilized for our marketing campaign, supporting the website that provides the services.

    Justification

    • To clearly identify the customers’ problems, our interview questions focus on both the social and psychological benefits that football brings to them, as well as personal life aspects that are football-related for hidden problems. 
    • The majority of our interviewees expressed these primary traits and concerns: 
    • Football was introduced to them from a very early age, mostly by male family members/friends or their favorite teams. Some stated that they were influenced by acquaintances who played professional football. 
    • They use social media platforms to stay updated about football news and events. They follow fan/official accounts of renowned football clubs on Instagram, and also use this platform to connect with other players and organize matches in local areas. Whatsapp is also utilized to contact teammates and regulate matches. 
    • Football provides them with a sense of community and accomplishment. The sport also aids them in socializing and stress relieving. 
    • Their frustrations include lack of equipment/fields, communication issues (hard to contact people/finding teammates through social media platforms, schedule flexibility, and lack of motivation due to the above difficulties.

      Lean canvas modifications

    • Customer segments- We realized that there’s a significant difference in lifestyles between age 18-25 and 25-30. The academic, professional, and social commitments will differentiate age 18-25 from 25-30 regarding football playing habits, spending habits, and availability. As a result, we modify our customer segment to age group 18-23 for a more focused brand identity and services. 
    • Problems- Based on the problem interviews, we were able to identify two main problems: Communication: The lack of a convenient and cohesive communicating platform prevents the players from finding available teammates, regulating matches, and looking for good facilities. Miscommunication causes a lot of disruptions in playing time which is already limited due to work and personal life. Schedule flexibility: The misalignment in availability makes it difficult for players to meet up and organize matches together. 
    • Solutions We came up with three brand features that potentially solves the identified problems: Availability input: The players are able to set up their schedules on the website to see which fields/players are available at a chosen time frame. Pitch booking: The feature introduces a list of available pitches for players to choose at a specific time frame. Digital baller passport: A digital passport for players to display their information and collect digital stamps given to them after each match. Match-making: The feature matches players with other players who are at a specific skill level and available at a chosen time frame. We expected these features to solve disruptions caused by the communication and flexibility issues as well as motivate players to play football.

    Summary of solution interviews 

    • Our solution interviews focused on confirming the problems and collecting reviews on our product prototype to see if we should pivot or persevere with the current direction. 
    • Highlights of the interviews: 
    • One interviewee expressed great frustration (laughing, talking with sarcasm) towards the inconvenience of having to contact people and synchronize schedules through Whatsapp. 
    • Most interviewees said they would benefit from a feature that helps them connect with compatible players. Some interviewees didn’t have the need for such feature, unless they wanted to meet new people. The feature will be helpful in small areas where you need to select players to make a team. 
    • The interviewees expressed enthusiasm about a platform where availability options are provided, because it helps them gather a team and organize matches more quickly and easily. 
    • The interviewees find it unnecessary for a skill level input feature, because to them “winning is important but fun is the top priority”. It depends on each player’ purpose of playing football. 
    • The interviewees looked forward to the feature where they can see the availability of the pitches which allows them to book in advance, because they’d prefer to have a familiar field to play regularly to having to switch fields every week.
    •  One interviewee stated that it would be helpful to add players’ (preferred) positions to their profile.
    •  One interviewee said that they really enjoyed the fact that you can see who you are playing with before hand and not find out on the spot. 
    • One interviewee said that we should include a second feature in which they can also invite friends to join their team.

    Conclusion 

    • The customers recognized the problems and found most product features helpful. We removed the skill level feature and decided to persevere with the rest.
