ABOUT US

We know how hard it is to schedule a football match where all of your friends show up. Navigating through the depths of time can be a challenge, especially when there are responsibilities to take care of. That’s where we, Ball4all come in! By using our platform you will be effortlessly scheduling matches whilst simultaneously booking the football pitch. Moreover, we aim to provide self expression both on and off the pitch with our Baller Passport.

Vision

In a world where life’s mundane tasks overshadow joyful hobbies, we believe that you deserve to reach the balance between them, only then you can find freedom within your passion.

Values

Flexibility At Ball4All, we understand the importance of balancing life's various commitments with recreational activities. Our platform is designed to offer maximum flexibility, incorporating features such as scheduling, booking, and group chats. These tools facilitate effective communication and organization, allowing users to easily integrate football into their busy lives.

Belonging The sense of belonging is at the heart of Ball4All. We strive to provide a safe and inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome to enjoy the beautiful game of football regardless of their skill level. The pillars of our community are respect, camaraderie and the shared passion for the sport.

Ease of use We aim to make our app as straightforward as possible in order to ensure the fact that everyone would be able to use it, since we believe that anyone is welcome to play football.

Freedom Ball4All values the freedom to play football on one's own terms. Whether it's choosing when and where to play, selecting teammates, or expressing oneself on the field, we empower our users to embrace the spirit of freedom that comes with the sport. We celebrate individuality, creativity, and the joy of unrestricted play within our football community.
